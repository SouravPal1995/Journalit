Journalit.org

Your own personalized journal

Journals are one of the oldest practices known to the human race. Journals allowed our ancestors to commit there experiences and memories to paper so that future generations could learn from them and build upon there triumps and failures. Maintaining journals have also been known to have therapeutic properties as it allows one to deal with their daily struggles and stressful experiences. Maintaining a journal is cost effective and healthier than spending countless hours of mindless internet surfing. Journalit is an opensource project that allows us to do just that without ever needing a physical paper. Also it's completely free!! All you have to do is follow the following steps. To contribute to this project visit the github

  1. Fill in your personal details
  2. Enter your name and email
  3. Set your password.

You are ready to go :)
